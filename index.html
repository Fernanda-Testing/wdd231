About Me

I've been testing software for almost four years, both for web and mobile applications—something I thoroughly enjoy. I'm passionate about ensuring the quality of what's delivered to the end user, especially when it's accessible to everyone. I enjoy challenges, and in the world of software, new ones are always emerging. Over time, I've realized the importance of evolving alongside technology, which led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Software Development at BYU-Idaho through Pathway Connect Worldwide. This experience has helped me discover how much I enjoy learning how software works and how to make it look more attractive and well-crafted. I believe that by studying and putting each challenge into practice, I can become a better QA professional and, in the near future, a better web designer.
