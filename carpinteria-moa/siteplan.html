Site Plan of Fernanda Souza

Site Name: CARPINTERÍA MOA

This name was chosen because the carpentry business began as a family business, and “Moa” was a very special pet in the family of Abayubá Borges, the founding carpenter.

Site Purpose:

The site offers a description of "Carpintería MOA" services as well as some examples of their completed work. It includes contact information and location. Provides a way for visitors to send us a message to request a quote, and also leave as a rating. It also showcases the carpentry's upcoming projects, and includes a section with tips for caring for outdoor wooden furniture.

Scenarios:

  • Where are you located?
  • Could you show me some examples of your work?
  • Do you specialize exclusively in carpentry or do you also offer other services?
