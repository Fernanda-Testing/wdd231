Abayubá Carpenter's Tip:

"Protect outdoor wooden furniture exposed to water and sun by applying a coat of a water-based wood protector (e.g., Cetol). It's recommended to do this every 6 months, or at most, a year to keep your work protected. Important: When performing maintenance, sand the wood first, then apply the protective product.
It's all up to the consumer's taste; if the wood is strong, like Lapacho, a simple sanding can give it a beautiful, rustic look."
